# ResuMind AI

## Description

Introducing AI ATS Resume Analyzer, an intelligent resume evaluation platform designed to help job seekers optimize their resumes and improve their chances of landing interviews.

Built with React, TypeScript, and Puter.js, the application leverages AI-powered analysis to evaluate resumes against industry standards and job requirements. Users can upload their resumes, receive detailed ATS compatibility scores, identify strengths and weaknesses, and get actionable recommendations to enhance their applications.

The platform simulates how modern Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) review resumes, helping candidates understand whether their resumes are properly structured, keyword-optimized, and aligned with the expectations of recruiters and hiring managers.

AI ATS Resume Analyzer provides:

* ATS score evaluation and breakdown
* Resume-to-job description matching
* AI-generated feedback and improvement suggestions
* Keyword and skills analysis
* Resume performance insights
* Secure and seamless user experience powered by Puter.js

Whether you're a student, a recent graduate, or an experienced professional preparing for your next opportunity, AI ATS Resume Analyzer helps you create stronger resumes and make data-driven improvements to your job applications.

Take the guesswork out of resume optimization and increase your confidence before hitting "Apply."
